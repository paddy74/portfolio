# Peersonal Grammar

This article documents reference material and personal preferences for crafting well-written documents.

I try to align with the general prescriptive American grammar whenever I am writing, whether it be technical, creative, or personal in nature. However, I am not a linguist, grammarian, or even someone paticularly exceptional at writing. So where I find my descriptive grammar and understanding of the appropriate vernacular to be lacking to convey meaning in the style I want, more authoritative sources are needed.

This article is my attempt to capture _my personal_ "correct" way to write &mdash; combined from my experience, education, and published authorities (e.g. the [American Psychological Association](https://www.apa.org/)) &mdash; so that I can continuously improve and share my motivations with colleagues.
